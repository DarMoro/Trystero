Our History

Founded in 2016 by Martin Trystar, TryStero has provided a trustworthy, speedy and cost-effective way to send and receive parcels, packages and anything that your business needs couriered. We have a dedicated team of drivers and excellent tracking and customer support. Based locally in Cork and 100% Irish owned, we believe in giving back to the community that has given us so much and we are proud to still maintain our root of opertions in the Port of Cork.

Our express was introduced in 2017 and since then we have been growing strongly with the help of our dedicated team and, of course, our satisfied customers. In August of 2016 we quickly moved from inter-county deliveries to international and introduced a whole host of services to help us get your parcels to the desired hands as soon, and as safely, as possible. We have been voted best delivery company in Cork two years in a row and look forward to growing and improving in every way that we can. We hope to grow with the city of Cork and are proud to facilitate them in every means possible while we do this.



Our drivers are as passionate and as dedicated as us and we are proud to have a continued success with over 97% punctuality and a 100% guarantee to deliver your good into safe hands. Transparency is very imnportant to us and we have a 24/7 tracking system with the automated and efficent updates that makes it easier to see extactly where your package is and where it has been.

We are delighted to introduce our new service, drone delivery, which will be launching in the new year. Further details will be available as the process progresses but we plan to have same day delivery, no matter where you are in the country, guaranteed. As ever, it will be unveiled soon and will be a reasonable and cost effective service with the customer and their needs at the heart of it. We look forward to sharing it with you.

We accept signatures at the door and use a unique tracking number so you can be assured and track your special delivery from our hands to yours. Log every express destination and map where your package has been. We hope to have the oppurtunity to do business with you and look forward to many years of continuing success, growth and partnership.
